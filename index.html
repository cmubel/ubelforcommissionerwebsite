Hello my name is Matt Ubel. I am running as an Independent for county commissioner. My roots in Pottawatomie County go back to 1853 when my family homesteaded north of Louisville. I am a farmer and a small business owner in the county. I serve on the board of directors as Vice President for Kansas Farmers Union. As commissioner my only agenda will be to serve my constituents and bring transparency to the citizens of Pottawatomie County.
